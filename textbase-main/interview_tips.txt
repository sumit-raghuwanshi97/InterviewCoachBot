Interview Tips:
1. Research the company before the interview.
2. Practice answering common interview questions.
3. Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.
4. Dress professionally and maintain good body language.
5. Showcase your skills and accomplishments using specific examples.
6. Be punctual and arrive at the interview location on time.
7. Follow up with a thank-you note after the interview.
8. Stay confident and positive throughout the interview.
9. Be honest about your experiences and qualifications.
10. Show genuine interest in the company and the role.
